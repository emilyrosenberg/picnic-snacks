All about vegetarian picnic snacks

Fresh and healthy, these snacks are great whether you're vegetarian or not. Try some homemade hummus, deviled eggs, and a unique and tangy watermelon salad!

Veggies and hummus

Homemade hummus is just better.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup dried chickpeas
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
  • Sprinkling of hot or sweet paprika
  • Raw veggies of your choice, cleaned and cut into sticks. Try carrots, cucumbers, zucchini, celery, bell peppers, or cauliflower or broccoli cut into florets.

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak the chickpeas in water overnight.
  2. Drain and place in a pan and cover with water by two inches. Bring to a boil and simmer until very tender, about 1 1/2 hours.
  3. Drain, reserving the cooking liquid.
  4. Transfer to a blender and add the lemon juice, tahini, garlic, cumin, and salt.
  5. Puree until smooth, adding 2 to 3 tablespoons of the cooking liquid as needed to obtain a soft, creamy consistency.
  6. Transfer to a shallow serving bowl and garnish with olive oil, parsley, and paprika.
  7. Serve with prepared veggies.

Deviled eggs

These tangy treats are worth the extra effort.

Ingredients

  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon dry mustard

Instructions

  1. Slice the eggs in half, removing the yolks.
  2. Mix the yolks with all other ingredients.
  3. Fill the egg whites with the yolk mixture using a small spoon.
  4. Chill 30 minutes to cool and set the filling.
  5. Pack them carefully for transportation, totally worth it!

Spicy watermelon salad

An easy and unusual fruit salad.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground red pepper, or to taste
  • 6 cups 1/2-inch pieces of seeded watermelon
  • 1/2 small red onion, diced

  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro or parsley

Instructions

  1. Stir the chili powder, salt, and red pepper together in a bowl.
  2. Toss all other ingredients together in a large bowl.
  3. Sprinkle with the spice mixture and toss well.
  4. Serve at room temperature - perfect for picnics!
